Vegan Char Siu Sauce Tofu

Description

This sweet tofu dish is delicious served with white rice, thin vermicelli noodles or stuffed into steamed dumplings.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegan hoisin sauce
  • 5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons Shaoxing pepper wine
  • ½ teaspoon five-spice powder
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha chili sauce
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 450g firm tofu, press to drain and cut into 2cm thick slices
  • Roasted sesame seeds for decoration
  • Chopped green onions for decoration

Directions

  1. Mix hoisin sauce, sugar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, wine, Chinese five spice and sriracha together to make the sauce. Set aside
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add tofu and saute until light golden, about 4 minutes per side
  3. Remove the tofu from the pan and let it cool for 5 minutes before cutting each piece into 2cm thick strips
  4. Reheat the pan, add the prepared sauce and stir until the sugar dissolves. Increase heat to medium-high and bring sauce to a boil
  5. Reduce heat when sauce boils. Stir well to keep the sugar from sticking to the pan and burning
  6. Add sliced tofu, mix lightly with sauce
  7. Fry for about 8 minutes, increase the heat to high and cook for another 2 minutes or until the sauce thickens
  8. Garnish the tofu with sesame seeds and green onions and serve hot
